About

This application is designed to assist in diagnosing skin diseases by using image recognition technology. Upload an image, and our system will provide a preliminary diagnosis and relevant information about the condition. This tool aims to raise awareness and assist healthcare professionals by providing a quick reference.

Please note: This application is not a substitute for professional medical advice. Always consult a qualified healthcare provider for diagnosis and treatment.
